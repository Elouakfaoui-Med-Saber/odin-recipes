Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are soft, fluffy, and delicious breakfast treats enjoyed around the world. They are simple to prepare and taste great with maple syrup, butter, fruits, or chocolate.

Homemade pancakes are perfect for breakfast or brunch and can be made in just a few minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups flour
  • 3½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1¼ cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Whisk together the milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients until smooth.
  4. Heat a lightly greased pan over medium heat.
  5. Pour about ¼ cup of batter onto the pan.
  6. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook the other side.
  7. Serve warm with syrup, butter, or fresh fruit.
