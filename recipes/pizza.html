Pizza

Description

Pizza is one of the most popular foods in the world. It consists of a crispy crust topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of delicious toppings.

It is easy to customize with your favorite ingredients and is perfect for family dinners, parties, or a quick meal.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Bell peppers
  • Mushrooms
  • Olive oil
  • Oregano
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Add mozzarella cheese and your favorite toppings.
  5. Bake for 12–15 minutes until the crust is golden.
  6. Slice and serve hot.
