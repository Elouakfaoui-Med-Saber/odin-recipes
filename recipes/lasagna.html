Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is one of the world's most famous Italian dishes. It consists of layers of pasta, meat sauce, cheese, and béchamel sauce.

It is rich, comforting, and perfect for family dinners.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles.
  2. Prepare the meat sauce.
  3. Mix the cheese.
  4. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes.
  6. Let cool before serving.
